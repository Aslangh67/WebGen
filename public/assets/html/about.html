About Us


WebGen is an app created by Robert, Aslan, Rafay, and Liam, who are students in University of Washington's Coding Bootcamp program. As a group, we decided to create an app that restaraunt (and possibly more) owners can use to build their own website and organize their business. This idea is scaleable enough that we could develop it into a desireable and effective app if pursued, allowing us to grow and learn as developers during the app's creation.
